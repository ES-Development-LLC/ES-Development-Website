Color Codes

Below are the main 3 colors of ES Development.
The HEX codes are below the colors.

ES Yellow

This yellow is the primary (main) color of ES Development. Most times, it is used specifically for links, header text, or other special items.

DO NOT use this color on a light background!

Hex: #F7DA26
RGB: (247, 218, 38)

ES Black

This dark blue is the secondary color of ES Development. Although it is secondary, you'll probably see it more often than our main yellow color. Use this dark blue for things like text, headers, or even as a background color!


Hex: #202A33
RGB: (32, 42, 51)

ES Grey

This grey is the alternative color to the dark blue. It's suggested that this color is used mainly for text on a light background.

DO NOT use this color on a dark background!

Hex: #0D0D0D
RGB: (13, 13, 13)
